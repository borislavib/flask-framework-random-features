Main page

This is the user part.

In Downloads you can download audio file from freesound.org. Registration is needed.

In Rating you can see the most added in favorites songs from the users. They can be downloaded or completely different. Registration is not needed.

In Listen you can listen to the audio files downloaded. Registration is not needed.

Favourites is for adding or removing favourite songs. Song and author is added. Registration is needed.

Users is a list with all the users. It shows the list with all the users and whether or not they share their favourites. No registration needed.

In Chat you can send messages to all the users online or just in a particular room. Registration is needed.

In the Logon/Admin users can register or login in the system. Also if you have the needed permissions you can log on as an administrator.

In the user part, if user is registered and clicks on his email(next to logout link) it redirects to the profile page for edit.
